Hyundai Ioniq 5

Price Hyundai Ioniq 5 price is ₹ 47.63 Lakh. When was the Hyundai Ioniq 5 launched? The Hyundai Ioniq 5 was unveiled in December 2022 and was launched on 11 January 2023 at the Auto Expo 2023. What variants does the Hyundai Ioniq get? The electric crossover is available only in a single, fully loaded variant. What features are available in the Hyundai Ioniq 5? Exterior: Exterior design highlights include the new parametric pixels LED headlamps, flush-fitting door handles, flared-wheel arches, and 20-inch alloy wheels. It also features an Active air flap (AAF) that improves aerodynamics when closed and cools vehicle parts when open. Interior: Inside, it comes equipped with two 12.3-inch screens – one unit each for the instrument cluster and the other for the infotainment system with navigation, ventilated front seats, Level 2 ADAS, power seats, climate control, eight speakers Bose premium sound system, ambient sounds of nature, and vehicle-to-load function (V2L) which is used via a power socket on the side of the car. What are the engine, performance, and specifications of the Hyundai Ioniq 5? The Hyundai Ioniq 5 is the first model from the brand to be based on the dedicated BEV platform E-GMP (Electric Global Modular Platform). Powering the Hyundai Ioniq 5 is a 72.6kWH battery pack mated to a permanent synchronous motor. The powertrain output is 216bhp and 350Nm of peak torque which is channeled to the rear wheels via a single-speed transmission. It gets an ARAI-certified range of 631km on a single charge and can be charged from 10-80 per cent in just 18 minutes using a 350kW DC charger. Is the Hyundai Ioniq 5 a safe car? The Hyundai Ioniq 5 gets Hyundai Smart Sense which ensures utmost customer safety. Additionally, it gets electric parking brakes, multi-collision-avoidance brakes, six airbags, a virtual engine sound system, and a power child lock.

KIA EV6

Price Kia EV6 price ranges between ₹ 64.39 Lakh - ₹ 69.62 Lakh depending on the variant selected. Variants The EV6 is available in two variant options, GT Line (RWD) and GT Line (AWD). Market introduction Kia India launched the EV6 in the country on 2 May 2022. Engine and Specification The Kia EV6 is powered by a 77.4kWh battery pack. The RWD version generates 223bhp and 350Nm of torque, enabling the vehicle to sprint from zero to 100kmph in 7.3 seconds. The AWD version produces 320.5bhp and 605Nm of torque. This top-spec version is capable of sprinting from zero to 100kmph in just 5.2 seconds. Both the variants get an automatic transmission. The EV6 offers three drive modes: Eco, Normal, and Sport, to suit individual driving needs. Exterior The EV6 measures 4,695mm in length, 1,890mm in width, and 1,550mm in height, while the wheelbase is rated at 2,900mm. The vehicle is available in five colour options, including Moonscape, Snow White Pearl, Runway Red, Aurora Black Pearl, and Yacht Blue. The EV6 gets sleek LED headlamps with DRLs flanked by two strong lines on the bonnet that taper down to the grille. The side profile is highlighted by a coupe-like roofline, flush-fitting door handles, and 19-inch diamond-cut alloy wheels. To complement the overall aggressive styling element, the Kia EV6 gets wrap-around LED tail lights with an LED light bar running the length of the boot lid. Interior As for the interior, the vehicle gets premium upholstery, a two-spoke steering wheel, and a large 12.3-inch curved touchscreen infotainment system. For convenience, the EV6 offers ventilated front seats, 64-colour ambient lighting, wireless charger, rotary dial with shift-by-wire technology, electric sunroof, electric parking brake with auto-hold function, and more. Seating capacity The Kia EV6 can accommodate five passengers.
